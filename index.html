Types of Coffee

  • Espresso: A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee.
  • Latte: A creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.
  • Cappuccino: Similar to a latte but with more foam, giving it a fluffy texture.
  • Americano: Espresso diluted with hot water, perfect for those who like a milder flavor.
  • Cold Brew: Coffee brewed with cold water over an extended period for a smooth taste.

Brewing Methods

Explore different brewing techniques to enhance your coffee experience!

  • French Press: A method using coarsely ground coffee steeped in boiling water.
  • Pour Over: A manual method that allows precise control over the brewing process.
  • Espresso Machine: Ideal for quick and rich coffee shots.
